🔥 About BlazeBites

Born from a passion for bold flavors and authentic grilling.

Our Story

WHERE FIRE MEETS FLAVOR

BlazeBites started in 2014 with one small grill stall in Lahore's food street. Our founder Chef Usman Zafar had one dream — to create fast food that actually tastes like it was made with love, not just speed.

Ten years later, we have 12 branches across Pakistan, a loyal customer base of 50,000+ food lovers, and a menu that keeps growing — but our original flame-grilled recipe has never changed.

We believe fast food doesn't have to mean boring food. Every item is handcrafted, every sauce is homemade, and every bite is built to make you come back.

Our Journey

2014

The First Flame

Chef Usman opens the first BlazeBites stall at Lahore Food Street with just 5 items on the menu.

2016

First Restaurant Branch

Opens first dine-in restaurant in Gulberg, Lahore. Wins "Best Burger" at Punjab Food Festival.

2019

National Expansion

Expands to Karachi, Islamabad, and Faisalabad. Launches the famous Blaze Sauce in bottles.

2022

Online Ordering Launch

Introduces website ordering and WhatsApp delivery. 10,000 orders in first month!

2025

50K Family Milestone

50,000 loyal customers and 12 branches. New items launching every quarter.
